about

Born of a desire to offset infectious pop hooks & lyrics with a modern rock intensity, Love Malison want to move you both physically & emotionally. With a London based lineup established in the throes of the pandemic, the songs can take you from lethargy to frenzy, & everywhere between. Catch the debut single, Flow, streaming on all major platforms August 6th.
